Drone Legislation in Spain

This page is a summary of the legislation for the Drones in spain
The legislation in Spain divides the drones into various categories, wi will resume the most practical an basic concepts and rules of each category:

Hobby Drones

The legislation considers the Hobby drones the ones that doesn't excide teh 2 Kg and are not used for professional activities.

For this category the main rules are listed in the following lines:

- The Drone must not exceed an altitude of 120m .
- We can just fly with Drones in adecuate areas as aeromodelism flight zones or rural depopulate areas.
- The damages caused by the drone will be responsability of the pilot.
- It's completelly forbidden to flight near airports nor aerodromes and places where other aircrafts will flight with low altitude.
- We can not put on risk any other people nor flight over people agglomerations as weddings, beaches or concerts.

Professional

This category will englobe all the drones that are used in business to deploy his work.

- The Drones that have a weight lower than 2Kg doesn't have the obligation of being registered on the aircraft register nor having an aeronavigation certificate.
- It's mandatory to add a plate to all kind of professional drones identifying properly the manufacturer of the drone and the legal data from the operator company.
- To make any operation in the air it's mandatory to request an official permission from AESA requesting the permission with at least 5 days.
- It's strongly forbidden to flight over urban nucleus nor spaces with a big people concentration without an special AESA permission.
- Each business must have a manual of operations following the standard provided by the ministry and a study of the security for each operation that is going to be realized.
- The designated pilots must have a official drone management license, if that pilots already have a pilot license it won't be necessary the drone management license
